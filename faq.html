Getting Started

How do I create an account?
  1. Click the 'Sign Up' button on our homepage
  2. Enter your email address and create a strong password
  3. Agree to our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy
  4. Check your email and click the 'Verify Account' link
  5. Complete KYC verification by uploading ID and proof of address
  6. Wait for approval (usually within 24 hours)
  7. Make your first deposit and start trading!
What documents do I need for KYC?

Required documents:

  • Government-issued ID (passport, driver's license, national ID)
  • Proof of address (utility bill, bank statement, government letter dated within 3 months)
  • Recent selfie for facial verification
How do I make my first deposit?
  1. Log in to your CTC account
  2. Navigate to 'Wallet' > 'Deposit'
  3. Select your preferred payment method
  4. Enter the deposit amount and complete payment
  5. Submit your payment reference in the portal
  6. Wait for approval (usually within hours)

Trading & Investments

What can I trade?
  • Stocks: Apple, Tesla, Google, Microsoft, Amazon, 10,000+ companies
  • Crypto: Bitcoin, Ethereum, Litecoin, USDT, USDC
  • Commodities: Oil, Gold, Silver, Natural Gas, Copper
  • Indices: S&P 500, NASDAQ-100, Dow Jones, Tech Index
  • Personality Stocks: Elon Musk Index, Bill Gates Index
How do I place a trade?
  1. Go to 'Marketplace' and select an asset
  2. Enter your investment amount ($10 - $20,000)
  3. Choose investment duration (60, 90, 180, 365 days)
  4. Review projected returns
  5. Click 'Invest Now'
  6. Track in your 'Portfolio'
How are returns calculated?

Returns are based on real market performance:

  • Entry price = price at investment date
  • Exit price = price at maturity
  • Profit = (Exit - Entry) × Quantity + bonuses

Withdrawals & Payouts

How do I withdraw funds?
  1. Go to 'Wallet' > 'Withdrawals'
  2. Enter withdrawal amount
  3. Select payment method
  4. Provide payment details
  5. Confirm and submit
  6. Funds processed within 24 hours
Processing times by method?
  • • Crypto: 30 mins - 2 hours
  • • Bank Transfer: 24 hours
  • • Card: 48-72 hours
  • • E-Wallet: 4-24 hours
